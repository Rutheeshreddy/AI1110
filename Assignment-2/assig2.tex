\textbf{21(b)[2019 ICSE 12$^{th}$]:}

The marginal cost function of x units of a product is given by 
MC = $3x^2 - 10x +3$. The cost of producing one unit is \rupee 7. Find the total cost function and average cost function.

\textbf{Solution:}

Let us denote marginal cost function by M(x), cost function by C(x) 
and average cost function by A(x).


\begin{table}[!htb]
\centering
\input{Tables/table1.tex}
\caption{}
\label{table:Table}
\end{table} 
 
 
 Given,
 \begin{align}
  M(x) &= 3x^2 - 10x + 3\\
  \implies  C(x) &= \int (3x^2 - 10x +3). \,dx\ \\
 \therefore C(x) &= x^3 - 5x^2 + 3x + k  
\end{align}

Where k is the constant of integration. \\
 Also given \,\,\,\, C(1) = 7 \\

 \begin{align}
\implies 7 &= 1 - 5 + 3 + k\\
 \therefore   k &= 8 
  \end{align}
  
   Hence,
 \begin{align}   
    C(x) &= x^3 -5x^2 + 3x + 8\\  
   A(x) = \frac{C(x)}{x} &= x^2 -5x +3 +\frac{8}{x}
\end{align}
